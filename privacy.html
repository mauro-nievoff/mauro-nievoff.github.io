Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This privacy policy explains how Mauro Nievas Offidani ("I", "me", or "my") handles personal information collected through this website (mauro-nievoff.github.io).

1. Information I collect

The only personal information collected is what you voluntarily provide through the contact form: your name, email address, and the content of your message. No other personal data is collected automatically.

2. How I use your information

Your contact form submissions are sent directly to my email inbox via Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form handling service. I use this information solely to respond to your inquiry. I do not sell, share, or use your data for any other purpose.

3. Cookies and tracking

This website does not use cookies for tracking or analytics. The only data stored in your browser is your language preference (English or Spanish), saved locally on your device via localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

4. Third-party services

Formspree: Form submissions are processed by Formspree. Their privacy policy is available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

GitHub Pages: This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server log data (IP addresses, browser type) as part of hosting. See GitHub's data collection policy.

Google Fonts: Fonts are loaded from Google's servers, which may log your IP address. See Google's privacy policy.

5. Data retention

Contact form submissions are retained in my email inbox for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and for legitimate business record-keeping. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data I hold about you. You also have the right to object to processing and to data portability. To exercise any of these rights, please contact me directly.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact me via the contact form on this website or through LinkedIn.

8. Changes to this policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
